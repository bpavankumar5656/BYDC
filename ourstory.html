Our Story

Our Story

We are driving the future of work by supplying varied technology talent on a large scale, bridging the skill gap and ensuring equitable access for all. At BYDC, we think that the technology skills gap is essentially caused by an opportunity gap. That skill and opportunity are not created equal. Every action we take is aimed at closing the opportunity gap. We create possibilities for people with the potential and attitude to be successful Software Engineers, as well as providing them with the necessary skills to be enterprise ready.

Mission

Our beliefs define who we are and what we do. We are more than just about fostering exceptional talent and technology at BYDC. Our objective is to pave the road for candidates with various experiences and educational backgrounds to realize their full potential as technology professionals, thereby empowering the next generation of technological innovation.

Vision

BYDC's Placement Services strive to be the finest partner for our clients' Recruitment Process Outsourcing and Learning and Development Program. Assist industries in locating the best talent for their specific needs. Improve staff competencies with our Learning Interventions.
